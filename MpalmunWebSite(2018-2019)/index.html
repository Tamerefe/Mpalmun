WELCOME LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

My name is Halil Kürşat Akkuş. This year as the first MPALMUN, I have gained the honor to be Secretary General. Which will be held between 26-28 April in Samsun, Turkey.

First of all I would like to thank our Director General Tuba Sarı our Deputy Secretary General Kübranur Bayraktaroğlu, our President of General Assembly Zehra Nur Saltan and every one of the members of the Organizaton Team.

As I have already mentioned, this year is the first MPALMUN held in Onur Ateş Anatolian Highschool also as known as Milli Piyango Anatolian High School. And we are expecting it to continue for many more years.

This year we will welcome you with four different committees three of which are General Committees and the fourth one is a Special Committee.

Today the Earth is suffering from various tragedies from child labour to the misusage of Nuclear Weapons. And the solution lies upon us, we shall make a better path for next generations for a brighter future. I welcome you all to the first edition of MPALMUN and I hope to meet with you all

Sincerely, Halil Kürşat Akkuş Secretary General of MPALMUN 2019
